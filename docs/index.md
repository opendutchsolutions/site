# Welcome

**OpenDutchSolutions** is a Dutch foundation focused on open software development, open hardware design, and sustainable repair of modern technology.

We build new systems.  
We restore existing ones.  
And when necessary, we analyze them to ensure interoperability and long-term usability.

Our goal is simple: technology should remain usable, maintainable, and understandable — even years after it leaves the factory.

---

## What We Do

### 🛠 Development

We design and develop:

- Embedded systems  
- Custom firmware  
- Hardware modifications  
- Interoperability solutions  
- Open tools for diagnostics and control  

See ongoing and completed work in our [Projects](projects/).

---

### 🔧 Repair & Restoration

We analyze, repair, and restore:

- Robotics platforms  
- Embedded Linux systems  
- Android-based hardware  
- Custom industrial electronics  

Detailed technical case studies can be found in our [Blog](blog/).

---

### 🔎 Interoperability & Technical Analysis

When documentation is missing or systems are locked down, we perform technical analysis to understand how components communicate — strictly to enable repair, reuse, or integration.

We focus on enabling compatibility and extending usability — not bypassing security or vendor protections.
